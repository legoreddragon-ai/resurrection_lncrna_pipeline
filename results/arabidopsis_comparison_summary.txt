Arabidopsis Sequence Conservation Analysis
==========================================
Query: 14 top desiccation-induced lncRNA candidates (C. plantagineum)
Database: Arabidopsis thaliana TAIR10 cDNA (Ensembl Plants release 57)
Tool: BLASTn, E-value thresholds tested: 1e-5 and 1e-3, word_size 7
Date: 2026-03-14

Result: 0 significant hits at both thresholds

Interpretation:
None of the 14 candidate lncRNAs show detectable sequence conservation
in Arabidopsis thaliana. This confirms these molecules are lineage-specific
to resurrection plants and are not shared with drought-sensitive model species.
This supports the hypothesis that resurrection plants have evolved a unique
set of lncRNA regulators distinct from those in non-desiccation-tolerant plants.
